---

## Format

Each entry records:
- **Component changed**: agent name, skill name, or command name
- **Nature of change**: Added / Modified / Removed
- **Behavioral impact**: What the AI will now do differently
- **Reason**: Why the change was made

This ensures anyone can understand not just *what* changed, but *why* agent
behavior evolved over time — a core principle of the CL4R1T4S approach to
AI transparency.
